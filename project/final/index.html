HOW DO YOU WANT TO HEAL?

“How do you want to heal?” is an introspective tool for community organizers and activists to self care. I attempt to create a more collective and holistic approach to self care, instead of mainstream materialistic movements that focus on bath bombs, face masks, and the like.
